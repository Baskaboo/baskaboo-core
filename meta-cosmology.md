## ⚛️ From E=mc² to Pits/Mits = (Klop/Laram)²

Modern science began decoding the universe with **Einstein's iconic equation**:

$$E = mc^2$$

By defining the speed of light ($c$) as the ratio of Space ($s$) over Time ($t$), the equation transforms into:

$$E = m \left(\frac{s}{t}\right)^2$$

Solving for the ratio of Energy over Matter, we derive the fundamental relationship:

$$\frac{E}{m} = \left(\frac{s}{t}\right)^2$$

In Baskaboo language:

- **Energy (E)** → **Pits** – the raw, unprocessed information, the dynamic current.
- **Matter (m)** → **Mits** – structured information, the vessel, the load.
- **Space (s)** → **Klop** – the step, the grid, the conductor.
- **Time (t)** → **Laram** – the void, the memory buffer, the collector.

Thus:

$$\frac{\text{Pits}}{\text{Mits}} = \left(\frac{\text{Klop}}{\text{Laram}}\right)^2$$
