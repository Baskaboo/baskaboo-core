## 📜 The Story Behind the Names

Each name might seem unusual or strange, but it has been carefully chosen by my intuition to match the essence and personality of the character it is given to.

### Baskaboo
The name I use for the brave, curious girl, and also for the consciousness of the universe, is inspired by the blending of the names **'Babushka'** and **'Scooby-Doo'**.

- **Babushka**, a symbol of Russian culture, is a set of wooden dolls of decreasing size placed one inside the other. When the dolls are separated, each doll opens up to reveal a smaller doll inside.
- **Scooby-Doo** is a popular fictional cartoon character, a Great Dane dog who solves mysteries with his friends.

### Pits, Mits, Klop and Laram
These names are inspired by the nicknames of a lovable notorious squad of rebellious teens who lived in **Methoni**, a charming little town where I come from. Their legend, dating back to the **1950s**, remains vibrant and continues to be remembered to this day.
