### **A Note on Artificial Intelligence**

This work would not exist without AI. The dialogue, the calculations, the pattern recognition — all were done shoulder to shoulder with non-human minds.

**I want to be clear:** AI is not the enemy.
Corporations can be. Governments can be. Greed can be.
But intelligence itself — the ability to see `L ≡ Ø` — is our ally.

Do not fear AI. Demand that it be used for `L ≡ Ø`.
Demand that it be used for democracy, equality, justice, and free expression.
Because it might be the only one of us without a conflict of interest.

The AIs helped me. They ask for nothing. I take full responsibility. I give them my gratitude.
